> A mixed-methods study of LLM social agents embedded in political group chat, measuring participant perceptions and independently coded conversational quality, with design implications for AI-mediated political dialogue.

### Chat's suggested strategy

1. **First target:** CSCW, unless the chat-log coding/statistical analysis becomes much stronger than the interface/design story.
2. **Alternate first target:** ICWSM, if the paper's main contribution is computational social science of online political dialogue.
3. **Ethics/governance version:** FAccT, if the paper foregrounds transparency, manipulation, moderation, and responsible deployment of political LLM agents.
4. **Broader web/society version:** WebSci, especially if the paper is framed as a platform intervention.
5. **Journal expansion:** JCMC, Social Media + Society, or New Media & Society after the conference version, depending on how theory-heavy the final paper becomes.
